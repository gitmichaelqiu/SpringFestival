The Origin

The Spring Festival, or Lunar New Year, marks the end of winter and the beginning of the spring season. It's the most important traditional festival in China, deeply rooted in history, honoring deities and ancestors.

Hongbao

Red envelopes (Hongbao) are a monetary gift given during holidays or special occasions. The red color symbolizes good luck and wards off evil spirits.

Lion Dance

Accompanied by crashing cymbals and beating drums, the lion dance is performed to bring prosperity and good luck. The loud noises are believed to drive away evil spirits.
